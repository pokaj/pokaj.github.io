Frequently Asked Questions

1 What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services, including deep cleaning, window cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and specialized commercial cleaning solutions.
2 How do you ensure the quality of your cleaning services?
Our team consists of trained and experienced cleaners who use professional-grade equipment and products to deliver exceptional results. We also conduct regular quality checks to maintain high standards.
3 Do I need to provide any cleaning supplies or equipment?
No, we bring all the necessary supplies and equipment. However, if you have specific products you’d like us to use, we’re happy to accommodate your preferences.
4 How long does a typical cleaning session take?
The duration depends on the size and condition of the space, as well as the type of service requested. We’ll provide an estimated time when booking your service.
5 What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?
We understand that plans can change. You can cancel or reschedule your appointment by contacting us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any fees.
